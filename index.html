How to Play:
The computer has randomly generated a sequence of colors. The objective of the game is to guess the computer's combination. Each turn, click the colored buttons on the left to add it to the sequence. Once you're finished, the computer will score your guess. Each black indicates that one color is in the correct position. Each white indicates that a color is present in the hidden combination, but is in the wrong position. A score of four black constitutes a win. Try and guess the combination in as few guesses as possible. Good luck!
